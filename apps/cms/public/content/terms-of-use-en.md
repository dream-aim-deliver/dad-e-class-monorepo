#### 2.1 Use of Cookies

Our platforms use essential and functional cookies to ensure basic functions such as login, security, and video playback.

**What are cookies?**
Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They make it possible to identify users and ensure the technical operation of the website. This allows sessions to be maintained and ensures smooth use.

We use the following types of cookies:

- **Essential cookies:** For example, authentication cookies containing user ID, name, email address, and avatar. These data are not shared with third parties and are used solely for identification during platform use.
- **Functional cookies:** These may originate from third-party providers (e.g., embedded video players) to ensure stable playback or an improved user experience.

Our cookies are encrypted and automatically deleted after a maximum of 30 days.
We do not use analytical or tracking cookies.
